Elect Her is Coming to RU!

This April, the Jane Addams Center for Civic Engagement is holding a workshop for Elect Her, a program run by the American Association for University Women (AAUW) and Running Start. AAUW supports the emphasis on women running for office, and Elect Her provides the training, encouragement, and empowerment that college women need to participate in student government positions, as well as political positions once their college career is over. The goal that AAUW and Running Start strive for is to eliminate the gender gap between persons in office. There has not yet been an Elect Her workshop in the state of Illinois, and we are honored to have been accepted to host one here at Rockford University. With the help of Faye Marcus, our Rockford AAUW representative, we hope to bring faculty and students of all genders from RU and other nearby campuses to participate in this event in April.

Community Partner Spotlight: RAASE

Rockford Alliance Against SexualAssault (RAASE) is the recipient of the 2014-15 Community Based Learning Community Partner Award. Throughout the Fall 2014 and Spring 2015 semester, RAASE worked with Human Development Senior Seminar students to address issues of sexual exploitation in the community.The mission and vision of RAASE is to seek to end the demand for sexually exploited individuals through preventative measures, victim protection, accountability of violators, and community engagement.To do this their main objective is to change the communitys perception of and definition of sexual exploitation through education and training sessions.
